# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in CodeOutcome a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability. We value
curiosity, precise technical disagreement, privacy, and respect.

## Expected behavior

- Be welcoming and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Respect boundaries and private information.
- Accept correction and take responsibility for mistakes.
- Do not publish another person's logs, source, credentials, paths, or database.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sustained disruption, and sharing
private information without permission are unacceptable.

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily
or permanently restrict participation when needed to protect the community.
Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact method listed
in the repository profile. Reports will be handled confidentially and fairly.
